The components part of this platform is NOT intended to be an exhausted supplement or alternative to Angular Material Components. Marching with Google, you should work hard to use established components per guidelines.

The components in this platform are composites or alterations of Angular Material Components as required by edge business cases. The components platform is then a handy place to share this work. The majority of work in this platform may be found in the fn and supporting services.

Care has been taken to keep a similar architecture to Angular Material. So you may install and use in the same manner.
