Make your services singletons by default. The reasoning is dependent on your app ( not platform ) architecture. In some cases services may be instantiated multiple times. This is commonly seen in lazy loaded modules where a service is added to more than one module. To prevent this issue, it is best to make your platform services singletons by default.
